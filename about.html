About this little world

I often wonder whether one day I might find my way to Europe and pursue a PhD in literature or philosophy.

I’ve always had far too many thoughts, about myself, about other people, and about the world. I love the feeling of tracing things back to their roots, of trying to understand why things happen and why we are here at all.

I’ve never been afraid to lay myself bare in the light, honestly and without disguise. At the same time, I know that no one is obligated to care deeply enough, or patiently enough, to wander through every hidden corner of my mind.

So perhaps this is a journey more than anything else. A journey inward, but not necessarily a private one.

That is why I created this page. It is a place for me to return to from time to time, to revisit the wild and wandering thoughts I once had. And perhaps, somewhere along the way, someone may care about my inner world enough to step inside and explore it with me.
