About this little world

Hi, I’m Julia. I think too much, feel deeply, and write things down before they disappear.

I built this website as a home for the thoughts that did not fit inside captions, conversations, or the tidy versions of ourselves we carry into daylight.

Here you may find stories about cities, ambition, love, friendship, uncertainty, and the odd tenderness of becoming someone new. None of it is a final answer. It is simply evidence that I was here, paying attention.

I hope you read slowly. Stay for a sentence, leave with a question, and take whatever light you need.
