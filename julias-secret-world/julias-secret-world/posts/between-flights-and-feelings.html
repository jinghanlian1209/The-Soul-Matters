Big Big World

Between Flights and Feelings

What airports teach us about leaving, arriving, and belonging nowhere for a little while.

January 8, 2026Somewhere over Europe6 min read
✈ delayed♫ boarding announcements♡ exhausted, enchanted

Airports turn everyone into a temporary version of themselves.

We sleep beneath fluorescent light, guard our passports like small countries, and trust a blinking screen to tell us where our lives go next.

Travel is romantic mostly in retrospect. In the moment, it is often missing luggage, cold platforms, expensive mistakes, and the stubborn decision to keep loving the world anyway.

I was tired enough to go home and curious enough not to.
