Does the Soul Really Matter?

On ambition, tenderness, and the parts of us that refuse to become practical.

July 1, 2026Los Angeles8 min read
☁ cloudy♫ Maybe it’s the affogato♡ brave, but soft

I once thought my dream was simply to live a happy life, and that the reason I pushed myself so hard was responsibility.

Then the people I loved gently lifted that weight from my shoulders and told me it was all right to rest. Without responsibility to hide behind, I finally saw what remained: ambition.

The world felt too large, and I felt too young to become smaller just because certainty was comfortable. That realization frightened me. It also made me honest.

Maybe the soul matters because it is the one part of us that cannot be optimized.
