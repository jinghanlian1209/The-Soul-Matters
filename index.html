A small archive of a very full heart

Thoughts collected from airports, sleepless nights, oceans, and ordinary Tuesdays.

I love collecting moments through photographs and words. Looking back, I realize how much they have quietly held for me along the way. Photographs have a way of making joy feel bigger. Words have a way of making me seem more sentimental than I probably am. Perhaps, even together, they reveal only a tiny fraction of who I really am. Welcome to my little corner of the world. I'll slowly fill this place with thoughts I've carried for years, alongside the new ones still taking shape. One piece at a time, I hope to write the poem of my life.
