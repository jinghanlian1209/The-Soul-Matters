Love & Souls

Outside the Order of Things

Because of you.

June 17, 2026Los Angeles2 min read

But how could I ever bring myself to blame you?

I only want to hold you gently,

to see if you have grown thinner from being tired,

to see if you have been sleeping well,

if you have been eating properly.

I know I have so many reasons to be angry.

And yet, the softest part of my heart begins to ripple,

until all that remains is tenderness,

and the ache of caring for you.

A mortal life lasts only a hundred years.

Love is a moment outside the order of things.

A line I want to keep

A mortal life lasts only a hundred years. Love is a moment outside the order of things.

Made with too many thoughts and just enough courage.
