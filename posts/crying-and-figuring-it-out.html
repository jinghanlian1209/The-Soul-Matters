Crying and Figuring It Out

A small defense of being emotional and functional at the same time.

June 30, 2026Westwood4 min read
☀ warm♫ kitchen noises♡ overwhelmed, still moving

Who decided crying means nothing is getting solved?

Some days I cry, get hungry, make noodles, answer the email, fix the problem, and continue crying. This may not resemble elegance, but it resembles survival.

There is a peculiar pressure to look calm while becoming stronger. I no longer think calmness is the only proof of strength.

Tears are weather, not a resignation letter.
