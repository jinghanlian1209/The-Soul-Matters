I Thought Today Was the Most

I beg you. If you don’t miss me. Don’t show up in my dreams again

July 3, 2026LAX4 min read

I suppose this is simply who I am, tangled and proud, writing *I miss you* a hundred times in my notes and never sending any of it to you.

But somehow, you know, don’t you?

Please stop coming to see me in my dreams. You know I think about you day and night. It is not that I don’t want to see you there. I do.

But every time I wake up, it takes me so long to dry my tears. My heart feels as though it has been cut open, and I am so tired of waking up in pain.

I used to believe that when life became difficult, facing it with someone else would always be easier than facing it alone.

I don’t believe that anymore.

Perhaps it is because I care about you too much. Beyond reason. Beyond control. Beyond anything logic could explain. Hopelessly. I lose myself in panic. Whenever I come close to you, something electric runs through my heart.

And that is exactly why I think I need to stay away.

I don’t want to reach for you like a lifeline while my own life is falling apart. I don’t want to hold on so tightly that the weight of me becomes too much for you.

That would be too heavy. Too unfair.

I know there are things I have to face on my own. I have to save myself.

You cannot do it for me, and I don’t want to pull you into the wreckage with me.

I know that if I come close to you, I will lose myself in you. But I cannot afford to lose myself yet. There are still too many things in my life that I have not put back into place. I know that both of us are barely keeping ourselves afloat.

I know I am someone who knows how to love deeply. But right now, I no longer have the strength to love another person well. I am afraid that would be unfair to you. And yet, I cannot bear to let you leave.

It is almost ridiculous.

If you knew, you would probably laugh at me in your head for the next ten thousand years. I even look twice at every Lexus that passes me on the road. Part of me hopes you are sitting inside. Part of me is afraid that you are.

I thought I could not possibly miss you more than I do today.

Unfortunately,

I thought the same thing yesterday.

A line I want to keep

I thought I could not possibly miss you more than I do today.

Made with too many thoughts and just enough courage.
